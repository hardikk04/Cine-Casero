Home Movie Day

The Family Movie Day is an open and free event that is held internationally and is organized by volunteers who seek to draw attention to the importance of preserving amateur records, daily and domestic for the value they have for the collective memory and cultural heritage of a country.

El Grton Film de Urugutoand

Inspection and enhancement project of the Carlos Alonso Fund Nitrates Collection.

Guarded by the National Archive of Image and Word – SODRE, it is made up of approximately 40 film cans. Filmed in various cities and landscapes across the country in the 1930s, in the form of a documentary record. The collection has not been studied in depth so far, therefore, the group intends to carry out an exhaustive technical inspection and photographic record to contribute to the diagnosis of the conservation status of the collection and its preservation. The Gran Film del Uruguay has the support of the UCLA Library's Modern Archives in Danger Program with funds from Arcadia, a charity fund by Lisbet Rausing and Peter Baldwin and the support of leading institutions in the country.

Nuestros servicios

We are professionals who come from the field of communication, audiovisual engineering and the preservation of audiovisual and photographic archives.

Advice

Storage, preservation and management of audiovisual collections in photochemical, magnetic and digital media.

Collection identification

We carry out identification, cataloging and diagnostic evaluations to preserve audiovisual collections. We seek to improve the preservation conditions of the supports to alleviate their degradation and we provide advice on possible ways of digitizing the material.

Digital file management

Design of storage structures, practices and according to the needs and requirements of each project. Classification and identification of material, access, back-ups.

Workshops

Management of personal and family audiovisual files, knowledge of tools and techniques for storage, preservation, preservation, access and back ups of material.

Film Performances

We design performative, analog and multi-screen audiovisual experiences, from found material (found footage) that are sound and musicized live. We link different materials and supports - 16mm, super 8mm, slides - that are reused in a new narrative and semiotic text, to generate new experiences and stories.

Research

Documentation of contemporary and historical audiovisual and photographic material for: academic and artistic research, documentary, fiction and audiovisual works in general.
